## 📌 Featured Projects

### OSHA Vision Auditor
A computer vision tool for workplace safety inspection, built at a hackathon. It uses a YOLOv8 model to detect OSHA violations from images and video in real time, then generates detailed violation reports with severity levels and remediation recommendations. Built with a FastAPI backend, a Next.js frontend, and Supabase for data persistence.  
**Stack:** Python, PyTorch, YOLOv8, FastAPI, Next.js, Supabase

### Speciation Simulation in Astyanax mexicanus
A stochastic C++ Monte Carlo simulation modeling speciation in the Mexican cavefish. By integrating population size, mutation rate, fitness, reproductive variability, and environmental pressure across hundreds of randomized trials, the model found that cave populations diverged in roughly 2,800 generations while surface populations required roughly 35,000, matching empirical findings in the literature.  
**Stack:** C++, Monte Carlo methods, statistical modeling

### The Quest for Prime Numbers (UIC MURL Research)
Undergraduate research through UIC's MURL program, supervised by Professor Evangelos Kobotis. Focused on turning mathematical ideas into running code, exploring algorithmic approaches for working with prime numbers and evaluating their performance at large scales.  
**Stack:** C++, algorithm design, computational efficiency
